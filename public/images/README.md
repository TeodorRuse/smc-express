# Image Placeholders for SMC Express

Drop your images here with these exact filenames:

## Required Images

| File | Used In | Suggested Content | Recommended Size |
|------|---------|-------------------|-----------------|
| `logo.png` | Navbar + Footer | SMC Express logo (transparent PNG) | 200x60px |
| `hero-bg.jpg` | Hero section background | Trucks on highway, warehouse aerial, or fleet line-up | 1920x1080px |
| `about.jpg` | About section | Team photo, office, warehouse, or company milestone | 800x600px |
| `fleet-0.jpg` | Fleet card 1 | Photo of vehicle type 1 | 600x400px |
| `fleet-1.jpg` | Fleet card 2 | Photo of vehicle type 2 | 600x400px |
| `fleet-2.jpg` | Fleet card 3 | Photo of vehicle type 3 | 600x400px |

| `coverage-map.png` | Coverage section | Europe route map with your actual routes highlighted | 800x600px |
| `testimonial-0.jpg` | Testimonial 1 | Client 1 headshot (or company logo) | 200x200px |
| `testimonial-1.jpg` | Testimonial 2 | Client 2 headshot (or company logo) | 200x200px |
| `testimonial-2.jpg` | Testimonial 3 | Client 3 headshot (or company logo) | 200x200px |

## Tips

- **hero-bg.jpg**: A wide, cinematic shot works best. It's displayed with low opacity (18%) as a background, so high-contrast images with trucks/roads look great.
- **about.jpg**: Something that tells your story — your first truck, your team, your HQ, or a behind-the-scenes warehouse shot.
- **fleet-*.jpg**: Clean side-angle or 3/4 photos of each vehicle type. Consistent framing across all 4 looks professional.
- **coverage-map.png**: A stylized Europe map with your routes/hubs marked. You can use tools like Mapbox or even a custom illustration.
- **testimonial-*.jpg**: Square headshots with neutral backgrounds. If you don't have client photos, company logos work too.
- Use JPEG for photos, PNG for logos and maps (transparency support).
- Optimize images before adding (use tinypng.com or similar) to keep load times fast.
